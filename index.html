ABOUT US
    7968 is Loyola University Chicago's cybersecurity club. We focus
    primarily on sending teams to competitions and training students to 
    compete. 

    We also host Cybersecurity related events, and partner with other clubs
    which have a focus on Cybersecurity and Computer Science. Join us or 
    follow us on social media to learn more.
