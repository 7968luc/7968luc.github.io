ABOUT US
    7968 is a Cybersecurity competition club at Loyola University Chicago. We focus
    primarily on sending teams to competitions and training students to compete. 

    We also host Cybersecurity related events, and partner with other clubs
    which have a focus on Cybersecurity and Computer Science. Join us or follow us
    on social media to learn more.
